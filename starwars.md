Star Wars - The Force Unleashed [Config.xml]
=========================================================================================
Add the following string 
```asm 
<s id="VideoWindowed">1</s>
``` 
right after the "VideoHeight" option
Setting "VideoWindowed" to 0 is equal to the fullscreen mode.
